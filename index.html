Services

  • Mental health counseling

    Mental Health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well being. Therapy services can assist you by providing support and better equipping you to face the challenges of life.

  • Psychiatric Rehabillitation Program

    Our psychiatric rehabilitation program is designed for those whose mental health condition makes it difficult to succesfuly integrate into the community and need additional support

  • Psychiatric Evaluation

    AWOC Provides a full range of Assesments to Accurately diagnos mental health conditions and determine the most effective combination of counseling and medication management services to minimize the impact on daily living.

  • Medication Mamangement

    Psychotropic medication management for those With diagnosed mental health conditions. Qualified professionals ensure appropriate dosage is recieved and monitor for compliance and effectiveness.

  • The Change Program

    The Change Program helps participants maintain healthier relationships. it consists of: (S.E.A.P) The Social and Emotional Awareness Program Aiming to Increase social awareness and emotional intelligence as well as (A.I.P) The abuse intervention Program focuses on eliminating domestic violence by increasing awareness and Accountibility

  • Substance Abuse Management

    `

    Away of Change provides substance use services including both intensive outpatient programs (IOP) and outpatient programs (OP) , As well as Suboxone maintenance. We have experienced professionals and curriculums designed to reduce or eliminate drug use.

  • Parole and Probation Compliance

    We provide a variety of services for those who have been mandated by the court and need to remain compliant. Services include anger management, gambling counseling, substance use treatment and more.

    Services for those facing DSS cases. We offer parrenting classes and DSS compliance assistance . Our Goal is to to help keep families together in happy homes
