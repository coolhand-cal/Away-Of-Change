    Our psychiatric rehabilitation program is designed for those whose mental health condition makes it difficult to succesfuly integrate into the community and need additional support

  • Substance Abuse Management

Current Patients

To remain active in any Away of Change program patients must attend at least one session each month. If A patient goes 30 days without a session he/she will be considered inactive. If a patient remains inactive for more than 30 days he/she will be discharged from the program and required to complete a new intake before resuming services.
